Planned Features

Customizability

Easily create your own look for AstroSound, using the built in theme picker or custom CSS.

LastFM Integration

Scrobble your played songs onto LastFM and let the world know what you've been listening to.

Streamer Friendly

Be able to output your currently playing song to a clean formatted text file to use within your streams and let your viewers know what hot song you're playing.

Favouriting

Save your favourite songs into a seperate playlist to easily find and listen to them again without having to shuffle through your library.

ID3 Editor and Viewer

Fix broken and missing ID3 tags on your songs. Never again will you wonder what album your favourite song is from.

Extensions

Build upon the base with your own extensions and add more features you think may be missing!
